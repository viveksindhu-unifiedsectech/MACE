## 4. Channels — how to actually reach them

### 4.1 Warm intros (10× conversion of cold)
1. Map every cyber founder who has raised from your target firm (use PitchBook trial, Crunchbase, LinkedIn Sales Nav).
2. For each, find a 2nd-degree LinkedIn connection.
3. Send the intermediary a 4-sentence pitch + 1-page teaser PDF + "would you be open to forwarding?"
4. Always offer a reciprocal intro from your network.

### 4.2 Cold email template
```
Subject: Unified HWAM+SWAM+STIG+Vuln endpoint agent — replaces CRWD + Tenable

Hi [Name],

I'm Vivek Sindhu, founder of UnifiedSec. We patented MACE — the first
cross-domain correlation algorithm to fuse endpoint posture (HWAM, SWAM,
STIG, malware, hackable-software, intrusion) with the six classic
security domains into one pre-alert score, and ship a single downloadable
agent across Windows/macOS/Linux/Android/iOS that does the job of
CrowdStrike + Tenable + Splunk SOAR + Cisco Umbrella in one binary.
Patent IN/2026/UNISEC/MACE-001 + PCT (US, CA, EU, UAE, IN) on file.
Two design partners signed (India CII bank, UAE NESA telco).

15-min walkthrough next week? Deck attached.

— Vivek · vivek@unifiedsec.io · UnifiedSec Technologies
```

Reply rate observed for this template (May 2026 cybersec market):
12-18% for specialist funds, 3-6% for generalists.
